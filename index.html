Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Beauty App (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) handles user information.

1. Camera and Face Data

The app may access your camera or photos in order to analyze general facial features (such as face shape, eye position, or lip outline). This analysis is solely for generating personalized beauty and makeup suggestions. We do not perform identity recognition or authentication.

2. Data Collection and Storage

All analysis is processed locally on your device. The app does not upload, collect, or store facial images or biometric data on any server. Temporary data generated during analysis is discarded immediately after use.

3. Data Sharing

The app does not share face data or personal information with third parties.

4. Data Retention

No facial data is retained after analysis. All processing happens locally and is cleared once completed.

5. User Rights

You may revoke camera or photo access at any time through your device settings. Since the app does not store data, there is nothing to delete on our side.
