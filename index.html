Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Beauty App (“we” or “the app”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

1. Information Collection

When you use the app's photo capture or photo library feature, we collect:

  • Photos: Full photos taken or selected by you.
  • Face analysis data: Feature data generated by Megvii (Face++) from your photos.

2. Use of Information

Photos are uploaded to Megvii (Face++) for face feature analysis. The resulting feature data is then sent to DeepSeek to generate the final analysis results. These results are used solely to provide personalized beauty and makeup suggestions.

3. Data Storage

The app does not have its own server. All analysis results and related data are stored only on your device. Data remains on your device until you delete the app.

4. Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following third-party services for analysis:

  • Megvii (Face++): Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek: Privacy Policy

Please refer to these policies for information on how these services handle your data.

5. Permissions

  • Camera: Used to take photos for face analysis.
  • Photo Library: Used to select photos or save generated posters.

6. Data Retention

All photos and analysis results are stored locally. No data is retained by the developer once you uninstall the app.

7. User Rights

You may revoke camera or photo access at any time through your device settings. Uninstalling the app will delete all local data.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: [chieh504@qq.com]

隐私政策

本隐私政策说明 Beauty App（“我们”或“本应用”）如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的信息。

1. 信息收集

当您使用拍照或相册功能时，应用会收集：

照片会上传到 旷视 (Face++) 进行人脸特征分析，返回的特征数据再传输到 DeepSeek 生成最终分析结果。分析结果仅用于提供个性化美妆建议。

应用没有自有服务器。所有分析结果和相关数据仅存储在用户设备本地，直至卸载应用。

4. 第三方服务

本应用依赖以下第三方服务完成分析：

  • 旷视 (Face++)： 隐私政策
  • DeepSeek： 隐私政策

  • 相机：用于拍摄照片进行人脸分析。
  • 相册：用于选择照片或保存生成的海报。

6. 数据保留

所有照片和分析结果均存储在本地设备，卸载应用后不会保留任何数据。

7. 用户权利
